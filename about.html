Personal & Portfolio Projects

I actively build and maintain portfolio projects to keep skills sharp and validate new tools before recommending them to clients.

One example is Catch Keeper—a custom application designed to help anglers log catches, track conditions, and organize fishing data. Projects like this provide a practical environment to test workflows, databases, UI patterns, and deployment approaches.

Portfolio projects are developed independently and do not interfere with client timelines or service delivery.

How I Work

I take a service-first approach: confirm scope, build with maintainability in mind, and deliver with clear handoff and optional support. If you’re not sure what you need, I’ll help you choose the simplest option that meets your goals.
