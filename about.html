About Maxwell Software Solutions LLC

Maxwell Software Solutions LLC was founded to help small businesses build and maintain reliable, affordable software solutions without unnecessary complexity. The company focuses on clean websites, practical business tools, and technical consulting designed to support real-world workflows.

Unlike large agencies, Maxwell Software Solutions LLC emphasizes clear communication, transparent pricing, and solutions that are easy to understand, maintain, and grow over time.

Personal & Portfolio Projects

In addition to client work, the founder actively develops personal and portfolio projects to continue learning and applying new technologies.

One such project is Catch Keeper, a custom-built application designed to help anglers log catches, track conditions, and organize fishing data. Projects like this allow new tools, frameworks, and workflows to be tested before being applied to client solutions.

Personal projects are developed independently and do not interfere with client timelines or service delivery.

Approach

Maxwell Software Solutions LLC takes a service-first approach. Projects are scoped carefully, built with maintainability in mind, and delivered with documentation and support options available.

Clients are encouraged to start small and expand over time, ensuring solutions remain affordable and aligned with actual business needs.
